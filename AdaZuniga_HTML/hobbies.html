Kickboxing:

I used to always be involved in dace classes an dance team back in my high school years but when I started college I wanted to try something different, so that is when I find out about kickboxing.
After some reaserch abuout the basics of kickboxing I decided to sing up for classes right away, and I am so glad I did. inmediately after the firts month of taking at least three one hour class of kickboxing per week I started to notice changes in myself.
Kickboxing has a lot of beneffits but the ones that I was able to experience and enjoiyed the most are:

  • burning a lot of calories
  • toning muscles
  • development of the respiratory and cardiovascular systems
  • decrease stress, and
  • improvement in mood
I have been on and off of kickboxing classes for 4 years now, for this reason I would like to build a small kickboxing studio once I aquire my own place.
